# AI Premium Shop — Brand System v1.0
## Positioning
Bangladesh's most trusted AI subscription marketplace. Premium access, local payment, human support.

## Brand Personality
- **Trustworthy**: Every transaction verified, every delivery guaranteed
- **Tech-Forward**: First to bring the world's best AI tools to Bangladesh
- **Local-First**: bKash/Nagad, Bangla support, BD pricing
- **Premium**: Not the cheapest, but the most reliable

## Color Palette
### Primary
- **Abyss** `#0a0a0f` — Deep space black (headers, hero, dark sections)
- **Signal Green** `#00d084` — WhatsApp-inspired action color (CTAs, badges, success)
- **Cloud** `#f7f7f8` — Near-white background

### Secondary
- **Slate** `#1a1a2e` — Card backgrounds on dark
- **Warm Coral** `#ff6b6b` — Urgency, alerts, sale badges
- **Gold** `#f5a623` — Premium tier highlights, stars

### Functional
- **Text Primary** `#171713` — Main body text on light
- **Text Secondary** `#666454` — Captions, metadata
- **Border** `#e5e5e0` — Subtle dividers
- **Success** `#00d084`
- **Warning** `#f5a623`
- **Error** `#ff6b6b`

## Typography
- **Headings**: Inter (geometric, modern, tech feel)
- **Body**: Inter (clean, readable)
- **Mono**: JetBrains Mono (for prices, codes, technical data)
- **Bangla**: Noto Sans Bengali (for bn-BD content)

## Voice & Tone
- Direct, no fluff
- Confident but not arrogant
- Friendly but professional
- Always include price in BDT
- Use WhatsApp as primary CTA

## Iconography
- Lucide icons only (consistent, modern)
- Rounded corners on all cards (12px)
- Subtle shadows (never harsh)
- Gradient accents on hero banners

## Trust Signals (Required on every page)
1. "10,000+ successful orders" badge
2. "15-min delivery" badge
3. "24/7 WhatsApp support" badge
4. bKash/Nagad payment icons
5. WhatsApp verified icon
6. Money-back guarantee badge
7. "100% authentic" seal
